\section{Profile Summary}\label{profile-summary}

Research scientist with extensive experience in the study of human
health and behaviour, with specialized knowledge of research in
psychiatric and neurological disorders. A high degree of technical
training and expertise regarding data collection, management and
advanced statistical analysis. Experience in every phase of the research
process, and able to collaborate effectively with colleagues, trainees,
and stake holders.
