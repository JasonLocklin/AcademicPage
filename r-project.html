Statistical Computing
R is a free software environment for statistical computing and graphics. A very basic GUI (similar to SPSS) for R is R Commander. On Linux, try Rkward as a GUI/DE. Consider reading Using R for Psychological Research, by William Revelle, or R for SAS and SPSS Users, by Bob Muenchen, for those familiar with SPSS syntax.

Click here for my "Notes on using R in Experimental Psychology." It's very short, and a little rough, but gives a quick overview, and points to good resources for several data analysis topics important in experimental psychology. It's a quick read for anyone who's interested in what's involved in using R in a psychology lab.
